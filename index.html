How to Cancel Your Subscription

You can easily manage or cancel your TravelR Pro subscription through Google Play Store. Follow these simple steps:

1

Open Google Play Store

Launch the Google Play Store app on your Android device.

2

Access Your Profile

Tap on your profile picture in the top right corner.

3

Go to Payments & Subscriptions

Select "Payments & subscriptions" from the menu.

4

View Subscriptions

Tap on "Subscriptions" to see all your active subscriptions.

5

Select TravelR

Find and tap on your TravelR subscription.

6

Cancel Subscription

Tap on "Cancel subscription" and follow the prompts to confirm.

Note: After cancellation, you'll continue to have access to TravelR Pro features until the end of your current billing period.
